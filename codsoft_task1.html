About Us

Royal Enfield is an Indian multinational motorcycle manufacturing company, headquartered in Chennai.

The Royal Enfield brand, including its original English heritage, is the oldest global motorcycle brand in continuous production. The company operates manufacturing plants in Chennai in India.
